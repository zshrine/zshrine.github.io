01. About

Seasoned Site Reliability / DevOps Engineer with 12+ years in IT. I have watched the industry evolve from monolithic on-prem deployments to stateless microservices in the cloud, and the shift from licensed software to SaaS — and I have engineered systems across that whole journey.

My focus is the reliability, availability, and scalability of complex systems: driving research and proofs-of-concept that improve performance and stability, building robust CI/CD pipelines, and optimizing operational processes. I thrive as a fast learner in dynamic environments, and I care most about turning operational noise into trustworthy signal.

Where I'm heading: having progressed from SRE to Senior SRE, I'm growing toward a Tech Lead role — leading large-scale technical solutions, expanding SME breadth, deepening automation and toil reduction, and driving Kubernetes-native monitoring as infrastructure moves to next-generation architectures.
